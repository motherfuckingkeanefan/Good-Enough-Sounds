My Recording Gear

My setup is simple and lightweight. I avoid obsessing over gear and focus on just pressing record. Here’s what I use:

Microphones

  • Line Audio CM4 – Small-diaphragm condenser with a natural sound.

Recorders

  • Zoom F3 – Offers 32-bit float recording, eliminating the need for precise gain settings.

Support & Accessories

  • K&M 23550 Microphone Stereo Bar – Supports the microphones in ORTF configuration.
  • K&M 85035 Microphone Holders – Securely mount the CM4s.
  • Line Audio CMEA Elastic Adapter – Isolates the microphones from vibrations.
  • Rode Tripod 2 – Compact and stable support.
  • Movo WST50 Windscreens – Reduce wind noise during outdoor recordings.
  • Tasker TSK 1026 Microphone Cables – For connecting microphones to recorder.
  • Manfrotto 015 Adapter – For converting 1/4" external thread to 3/8" internal thread.

Recording Approach

I keep my setup as lightweight and simple as possible. The goal isn’t to achieve perfect recordings—it’s to capture real soundscapes, with all their natural imperfections.
