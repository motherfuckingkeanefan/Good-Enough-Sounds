About This Project

My name is Pieter Houtekamer. I started Good Enough Sounds as a way to stop overthinking and start recording.

The Problem with Perfection

Like many beginners, I spent more time researching field recording than actually doing it. I convinced myself that I needed better gear, more knowledge, and the perfect conditions before I could start. But waiting for perfection meant never recording at all.

Recording Without Fear

This project is about embracing imperfection. Instead of chasing flawless recordings, I focus on capturing sound as it happens—wind, background noise, and all. The goal is to document reality, not edit it.

Where I Record

Most of my recordings are made around Katwijk aan Zee, a coastal town in the Netherlands. The sea, dunes, and changing weather provide an unpredictable soundscape. I also record in nearby wetlands and quiet rural areas.

Approach

I use simple, reliable equipment and minimal post-processing. The point isn’t to make perfect recordings—it’s to capture what I hear, when I hear it.

Good Enough Sounds is a personal project, but I hope it resonates with others interested in sound, field recording, or just getting past perfectionism.
