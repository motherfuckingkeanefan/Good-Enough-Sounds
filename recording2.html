Morning Birds in the Dunes

Location and Conditions

Recorded in the dunes near Katwijk, a quiet natural area with open grasslands and small patches of forest. The morning was clear, and the temperature was low, creating crisp, natural acoustics.

Recording Setup

The microphones were set in ORTF configuration and placed at a slight elevation to capture the open soundscape. The location was chosen for its distance from human activity, allowing for an undisturbed morning chorus.

Technical Details

  • Coordinates: 52.2061° N, 4.3855° E
  • Date: February 2025
  • Time: 06:45 CET (sunrise)
  • Weather: 5°C, clear sky, very light wind (3 km/h SE)
  • Microphones: Line Audio CM4 (ORTF)
  • Recorder: Zoom F3 (32-bit float, 48 kHz)

Listen

Observations

The early morning conditions allowed for a natural and balanced recording of bird activity. The blackbirds and finches were most prominent, with occasional calls from crows in the background.

The absence of wind made for a clean recording, with only subtle environmental noise from distant trees. This session highlights the dynamic changes in bird activity as the sun rises.
