Welcome to Good Enough Sounds

Field recording can feel overwhelming. There's always more to learn, better gear to buy, and a voice in your head telling you the recording isn’t good enough. I created Good Enough Sounds to break that cycle.

Good Enough Sounds is about capturing the raw, authentic sounds of the world without endless tweaking or waiting for perfect conditions—just the act of pressing record.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Authentic recordings of the Dutch coast, dunes, and natural environments.
  • Detailed notes on location, weather, and recording conditions.
  • Insights and experiments with different microphone techniques.
